Our Story

Family-Owned, Community-Rooted

K&J Caribbean Market and Restaurant is a family-owned business located at 7629 N 56th Street in Tampa, Florida. We are proud to serve the Caribbean community and everyone who loves authentic island flavors.

Whether you're stocking up on your favorite Caribbean ingredients or sitting down for a hot plate of griot, bouillon, or oxtail — you'll always feel at home with us.
